WHO WE ARE

◯ Our Story

Born from great coffee, loud music, and people who don't do things halfway.

We're not a corporate chain. We're an independent coffee shop run by a small team that takes coffee — and the people drinking it — seriously. Everything here is done deliberately: the beans, the playlist, the way we say hello in the morning.

The Burnout Society is for the early starts, the late nights, the deadline weeks, the days you just need a really good drink and somewhere that feels like yours. Pull up a chair. The riffs are loud, the lights are low, and the coffee is exactly the way it should be.

Sleep when you're dead.

◯ Behind The Counter

The people who make TBS feel like TBS.

Ashleigh, Eric, Thandi — and the rest of the crew — pull the shots, run the floor, and treat every regular like one of their own. Warm, no-nonsense, and genuinely happy you walked in. That's the whole thing.
